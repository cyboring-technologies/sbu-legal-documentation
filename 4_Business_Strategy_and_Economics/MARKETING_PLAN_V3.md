# Marketing Plan — SBU-Legal

**Context:** *Strategic document outlining the organic acquisition architecture and hypothesis for SBU-Legal.*

# 0. Structural Scope & Governance

## 0.1 Structural Freeze Statement
* This document is a strategic acquisition layer.
* It does not alter architecture.
* It does not change UX contracts.
* It exists entirely subordinate to the Engine's operational reality.

## 0.2 External Authority References
* Subordinate to `BUSINESS_AXIOMS.md`.
* Subordinate to `UX_E2E_CONTRACT.md`.
* Acts within parameters of `MARKETING_PARAMETRIC_MODEL.md`.

## 1. Strategic Principle
* Acquisition is purely transactional.
* Users buy a single document execution.
* Growth is a function of transaction volume.
* There is no retention.

## 2. Core SEO Hypothesis
* Direct procedural intent captures immediate demand.
* High structural margins (>90%) allow SEO to be the primary compounding acquisition channel.
* The one-shot model, lacking LTV, necessitates scalable, low-CAC organic acquisition.
* Explicit trade-off: SEO provides latency but eventual autonomy.
* Explicit trade-off: Ads provide immediate speed but carry controlled financial risk.
* Paid ads provide probabilistic validation, not long-term structural foundation.

## 3. Keyword Strategy (Long-Tail Procedural Intent)

### 3.1 Structural Keyword Pattern
* Explicit pattern: [Procedural Verb] + [Document] + [Jurisdiction].
* Informative intent (e.g., general definitions) is discarded.
* Procedural intent (e.g., drafting a specific response) is prioritized.
* Every keyword must map directly to a supported Engine capability.
* Discard generic educational terminology.

## 4. Landing Architecture

### 4.1 Dedicated Procedural Landing Pages
* Each landing page must map 1:1 to a specific procedural act.
* Each page must lead directly and unconditionally to the engine.
* Pages must not become lengthy, discursive educational content.
* Strict architectural separation: landing pages inform and cede control.
* Do not convert pages into onboarding flows.

### 4.2 No Blog as Traffic Dependency
* Avoid content without a direct transactional exit.
* Content must align strictly with procedural execution.

## 5. Content Production Cadence

### Month 0--2:
* Rapidly establish the initial indexable surface area.
* Achieve minimum viable initial saturation to begin indexation delay.
* Prioritize high-urgency procedural tasks.

### Month 3--5:
* Maintain steady inventory production.
* Accumulate structural compounding and organic visibility over time.

### Month 6--9:
* Optimize the index strictly based on algorithmic signals (impressions, CTR, indexing rates).
* Adjust production variables based on empirical traffic analysis.

## 6. Technical SEO Requirements
* Maximize indexability.
* Ensure absolute crawlability of procedural pages.
* Maintain strict technical neutrality.

# 7. Structural Financial Logic (Reference Model Only)

## 7.1 Dominant Hypothesis (Baseline Selected)
* Capitalizes on organic inventory expansion.
* Utilizes high initial gross margins for runway.

## 7.2 Timeline of Structural Phases
* Latency period absorbed while organic indexation matures.
* Probabilistic acceleration deployed concurrently.

## 7.3 CRO Activation Phase
* Optimize existing traffic systematically.
* Semantic structuring and friction reduction applied to stable pages.

## 7.4 Geographic Expansion Trigger (Aligned with CITY_EXPANSION_MONTH)
* Execute geographic scaling only upon validation of the core baseline.
* Duplicate successful architecture across new jurisdictions.

# 8. Structural Abandonment & Escalation Signals

## 8.1 Execution Framework Kill Criteria (Referenced, Not Redefined)
* Overarching kill criteria dictate abandonment independently of this plan.

## 8.2 SEO-Specific Structural Failure Signals
* Temporary threshold of zero significant impressions breached.
* Persistently unreasonable CTR on exact match queries despite top rankings.
* Increasing reliance on paid ads to sustain expected transaction volume.
* Failure to achieve projected organic indexation within operational horizons.

## 8.3 Ads Stop-Loss Governance
* Ad spending permanently halts upon hitting the defined stop-loss limit.
* Unprofitable paid acceleration will not be subsidized.

## 9. Operational Philosophy
* Absolute discipline regarding feature introduction: no feature may alter the one-shot model.
* Explicit rejection of newsletters, lead magnets, and pre-execution email captures.
* Any deviation from these targeted rejections constitutes the creation of a different business.
* Disciplined execution of search inventory expansion.
* Total focus on absolute transaction volume.
* Rejection of brand narratives and emotional storytelling.

# 10. Structural Financial Scenarios

## 10.1 Hypothesis Selection Rule (Operational Default)
* The Baseline Scenario governs daily operational allocation.

## 10.2 Pessimistic Scenario
* Assumes sluggish indexation.
* Restricts capital deployment strictly to survival constraints.

## 10.3 Baseline Scenario
* Steady inventory growth yielding median metric performance.
* Normal activation of optimization and indexation phases.

## 10.4 Optimistic Scenario
* Rapid organic metric traction.
* Accelerates timelines for geographic and service expansion.

## 10.5 Operational Decision & Migration Rule
* Scenario shifts occur strictly when explicit metric triggers are breached.

# 11. Structural Integrity & Governance Lock
* The structural rules established herein are fixed.
* Modification requires a formal overriding business proposition.
* Completely bound to the underlying one-shot execution model.

# 12. Conclusion
* SBU-Legal is a strictly transactional architecture dependent on exact procedural intent.
* Execution is one-shot, irreversible, and inherently devoid of lifecycle retention.
* Survival is predicated on structural margins isolating the business from traffic volatility.
* Any attempt to build continuity breaks the economic and operational foundation of this unit.
