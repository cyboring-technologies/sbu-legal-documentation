# Business Axioms & Operating Model

**Context:** *Establishes the non-negotiable business rules. It defines the product as a single irreversible execution and prohibits any UX features that imply accounts, saving, or recurring use.*


**STATUS:** AUTHORITATIVE · SINGLE SOURCE OF TRUTH  
**SCOPE:** Business Meaning · Value Model · UX Guidance  
**SUPERSEDES:** Business Plan v1.x (Legacy, Non‑Applicable)

---

## 1. What This Document Is

This is **not** a marketing plan.  
This is **not** a growth roadmap.  
This is **not** a SaaS pitch.

This document defines the **business axioms** of SBU‑Legal as it actually exists today.

If a proposal, UX decision, copy change, or feature idea contradicts this document, **the proposal is invalid by definition**.

---

## 2. Core Business Thesis

SBU‑Legal sells **one thing only**:

> **An irreversible legal document artifact, generated once, and then destroyed at source.**

We do **not** sell:
- access
- subscriptions
- continuity
- accounts
- platforms
- workspaces
- tools over time

The product exists **only for the duration of execution**.

---

## 3. Value Definition

### 3.1 What the Client Buys

The client buys:
- one execution
- one document
- one irreversible transformation
- one downloadable artifact

The value is **entirely contained in the downloaded file**.

There is no residual value inside the system.

---

### 3.2 What the Client Does *Not* Buy

The client does not buy:
- storage
- history
- retries
- revisions over time
- “using the software”
- an ongoing relationship

The system is **not a service**.  
It is a **machine**.

---

## 4. Payment Semantics

Payment is **not**:
- access
- membership
- credits
- balance
- entitlement

Payment is **authority**.

> Payment authorizes exactly one irreversible execution.

Once authority is consumed:
- it cannot be reused
- it cannot be restored
- it cannot be extended

---

## 5. Execution Model (Business View)

### 5.1 One‑Shot Invariant

Each session allows:
- zero or one final execution
- never more than one

No retries.  
No partial success.  
No continuation.

---

### 5.2 Incineration Invariant

After completion or failure:
- all inputs are destroyed
- all metadata is destroyed
- all intermediate artifacts are destroyed

Nothing remains.

This is not a privacy feature.  
It is the **business model itself**.

---

## 6. UX & Meaning Constraints (Business‑Level)

The system must **never** imply:
- continuity
- accounts
- saving
- returning later
- “your documents”
- “your workspace”

Any UX, copy, or flow that creates these expectations is a **business violation**, not a UX bug.

---

## 7. Revenue Model

### 7.1 Unit of Sale

**Unit:** One execution  
**Outcome:** One downloadable legal document

There are no bundles at the business‑logic level.  
Any pricing abstraction must collapse to this unit.

---

### 7.2 Cost Alignment

Costs are incurred:
- only during execution
- only while value is being created

Idle system cost trends toward zero.

Revenue and cost are temporally coupled.

---

## 8. What This Business Is NOT

SBU‑Legal is explicitly **not**:
- a SaaS platform
- a legal workspace
- a document management system
- a subscription product
- an AI assistant
- a productivity suite

Any future proposal that moves in these directions constitutes a **new business**, not an evolution of this one.

---

## 9. Strategic Consequence

Because the system:
- does not retain users
- does not retain data
- does not build accounts
- does not accumulate history

**Trust is created by absence, not presence.**

Reliability is demonstrated by:
- correctness
- restraint
- deletion
- finality

---

## 10. Governance Rule

This document has authority over:
- UX meaning
- copy framing
- pricing semantics
- flow design
- agent instructions

It does **not** dictate:
- UI design
- visual identity
- wording choices
- implementation details

---

## 11. Final Axiom

> **SBU‑Legal is not a product you use.  
It is an execution you survive with a document in hand — and nothing else left behind.**
