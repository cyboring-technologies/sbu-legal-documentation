# Marketing Plan — One-Shot Model

**Context:** *Outlines the go-to-market strategy, focusing exclusively on Google Search Ads to capture high-intent traffic. It explicitly bans social media ads and brand-building campaigns.*

## 1. Strategic Principle

This business does not compete for attention.  
It competes to **resolve active intent**.

Therefore:

- Primary Channel: **Google Search Ads**
- Secondary Channel: **SEO via specific action landing pages** (only after validation)
- No Meta Ads or discovery campaigns are used.

### Initial Core Product & Geographic Focus

1.  **Core Product:** First validated service → **Lawsuit Response Generation**.
    - The system does NOT launch multiple core services in parallel.
    - Contracts are not tested in this phase.
    - Validation is sequential.

2.  **Mandatory Geographic Segmentation:**
    - The campaign starts in **ONE single jurisdiction**.
    - Broad regional segmentation (LatAm) is not allowed.
    - Multiple countries are not allowed in Month 1.
    - Geographic expansion occurs only after validating CR ≥ 7%.

---

# 2. Main Channel: Google Search Ads

## Conversion Definition & Funnel Logic

Define explicitly:

Primary Conversion:
* Successful Payment Finalization (Rubicon crossed).

Secondary Funnel Metrics:
* Upload Rate
* Quote Acceptance Rate
* Payment Completion Rate
* Draft Download Rate

Clarify:
* All CR references refer to Payment Conversion Rate unless specified.
* Uploads without payment are not considered success.
* Optimization prioritizes Payment CR above all.

## Objective

Buy real intent and validate:

- Keywords with active pain (transactional urgency).
- Correct messaging.
- Real conversion (CR).
- Economic viability.

## Keyword Strategy

- **Intent type:** Urgent transactional.
- **Match types:** Exact + Phrase (No broad match in phase 1).
- **Maximum:** 20 initial keywords.

**Structural Base Keywords:**
- “lawsuit response”
- “answer to lawsuit”
- “lawsuit response model”
- *[procedural variants according to selected country]*

## Campaign Structure Protocol (Month 1)

Mandatory structure:
* 1 Campaign
* 1 Jurisdiction
* 2–3 Ad Groups
* 5–10 keywords per group
* Exact + Phrase only
* No Broad match
* No Display Network
* No Search Partners (initially)

Weekly enforcement:
* Search term mining
* Add negative keywords
* Pause keywords with ≥ 20 clicks and 0 conversions

---

## Budget and Strategy (Month by Month)

### Month 1 — Discovery ($500)
- **Objective:** Identify live keywords in a single jurisdiction.
- **Landing Strategy:**
  - Traffic directed to the **Homepage**.
  - Surgical H1/H2 adjustment aligned to the chosen keyword.
  - **NO specific landing page is created yet.**

### Homepage Intent Alignment Requirement

Before launching Ads:
* Hero H1 must contain the primary keyword verbatim.
* Subheadline must clarify the specific action.
* Abstract institutional positioning must be minimized during validation.

Homepage is temporarily optimized for intent validation, not brand positioning.

- **Metrics:** Profitability is not evaluated, only intent signal.

### Month 2 — Confirmation ($500–600)
- **Dedicated Landing Criteria:**
  - A dedicated transactional landing page is created ONLY if the keyword shows:
    - ≥ 30–50 clicks
    - CR ≥ 5%
    - Consistent signal
- If metrics are not met, adjust the homepage or keywords, not a new landing page.
- Prune weak keywords.

### Month 3 — Break Even ($500–700)
- Optimize validated landing page.
- Start SEO indexing.
- Objective: reach break-even.

### Month 4–6 — Initial Profitability ($400–600)
- SEO starts contributing traffic.
- Ads stop being oxygen and become an accelerator.

### Month 6–12 — Optimization ($300–500)
- Ads only for new actions, new jurisdictions, or price tests.

---

## Validation Metrics (Month 1 Success)

- **Target CR:** ≥ 5% minimum
- **Signal:** Sufficient to justify building a dedicated landing page.

### Economic Viability Rule

* CPC is evaluated relative to CPA viability.
* CPA must remain ≤ 50% of ticket.
* No fixed CPC ceiling exists.
* Higher CPC is acceptable if unit economics remain viable.

## Economic Termination Rules

Keyword pause condition:
* ≥ 20 clicks
* 0 conversions
* OR CPA > 50% ticket

Campaign pause condition:
* ≥ 150 clicks
* CR < 3%

Data governs continuation.
No emotional attachment to keywords.

# 3. Secondary Channel: Structural SEO

## Focus

No massive blog.  
SEO = **Intent Capture Surfaces**.

## Operational Rule

A new landing page is created only when:

- The keyword obtains 30–50 accumulated clicks in Ads.
- It demonstrates clear signals of intent.
- It has CR ≥ 5%.

## Correct Flow

1. Keyword validated in Ads.
2. Specific action landing page is built.
3. Direct ONLY that keyword to the new landing page.
4. If CR improves → index consciously.
5. Internal linking.
6. Progressive organic capture begins.

---

# 4. Why Meta Ads Are Not Used

## Strategic Reasons

1. Product based on active intent.
2. It is not a discovery model.
3. Identity or emotional brand is not being sold.
4. There is no subscription or retention.
5. High creative friction (video, design, constant testing).
6. Low alignment with the one-shot model.

---

# 5. Service Expansion Strategy

## Horizontal Independence (No Sequences)

The system supports 8 totally independent and parallel procedural transformations (Initiate Process, Answer/Oppose, Appeal, Request Measure, Modify, Execute, Inform, Withdraw). 

A user does **not** proceed through these sequentially. A lawyer may arrive solely to buy an Appeal draft, while another arrives solely to initiate a lawsuit. Each service is an isolated, one-shot transaction matching a specific, immediate intent.

## Advertising Validation Rhythm

While the 8 services exist independently in the Engine, **Marketing Ad validation** must still be focused to conserve budget:

- **Ad Target 1 (e.g., Lawsuit Response):** Validate intent and conversion first.
- **Ad Target 2 (e.g., Appeals):** Launch ads only when Target 1 is profitable.
- **Ad Target 3 (e.g., Initial Lawsuits):** Launch ads only when 1 and 2 sustain structure.

Never launch Google Ads for all execution modes in parallel during the validation phase. Validate one intent channel at a time.

---

# 6. Expansion Contingency Model

Expansion occurs only if:
* CPA ≤ 50% ticket
* CR ≥ 5%
* Consistent signal across ≥ 30–50 clicks

No revenue projections without live validated data.

---

# 7. Language & Jurisdiction Alignment

* Campaign language must match jurisdiction language.
* Spanish-speaking jurisdiction → Spanish ads + Spanish homepage.
* No bilingual testing in Month 1.
* No mixed-language campaigns during validation phase.

---

# 8. Operational Philosophy

- Ads buy learning.
- SEO consolidates independence.
- Price is not lowered to improve conversion.
- Kill quickly what doesn't work.
- Scale only what converts.

---

# 9. Conclusion

This plan:

- Minimizes cognitive risk.
- Maximizes fast truth.
- Reduces progressive Ads dependence.
- Allows structured expansion.
- Builds a long-term sustainable asset.

Virality is not sought.  
Real intent, high conversion, and structural profitability are sought.
