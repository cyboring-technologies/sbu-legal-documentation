# AntiPages — Master Inventory

## Brief Document Description

Structural document that defines the complete annual inventory of AntiPages (parametric landing pages) for SBU-Legal.

**Its function is to:**
* Map services × jurisdictions × variants.
* Establish prioritization by Tiers.
* Differentiate between total inventory and progressive activation.
* Serve as the source for generating the technical dataset (`antipages.json`).

> [!NOTE]
> This does not imply immediate full deployment.

**This document:**
* Does not contain developed copy.
* Does not contain code.
* Does not define a monthly timeline.
* Does not redefine strategy.
* Does not modify the business model.

**It is exclusively:**
A structural map of planned SEO footprint until December 2026.
