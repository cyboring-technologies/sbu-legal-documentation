# GSC Indexation Playbook — SBU-Legal

## 1. Objective

Define the recurring operational protocol to accelerate indexation of AntiPages after deployment.

---

## 2. Scope

Applies to:

* Newly deployed AntiPages (monthly batches)
* Existing pages not yet indexed

Excludes:

* Technical SEO changes
* Architecture or sitemap modifications

---

## 3. Input

* Source: AntiPages inventory (Tier A / B)
* Monthly deployment: 8–12 pages per batch

---

## 4. Core Loop (Execution Protocol)

Repeat per cycle:

### Step 1 — Select URLs

* Select 5 URLs
* **Only URLs with status = 200**
* Priority:

  1. JUR_1
  2. Variant V1
  3. Transactional intent pages

---

### Step 2 — Request Indexation (GSC)

For each URL:

* Open Google Search Console
* URL Inspection
* Request Indexing

Output:

* 5 URLs submitted

---

### Step 3 — Backlink Trigger

* Create 1–2 backlinks per cycle

Valid sources:

* GitHub README (preferred)
* Notion public page
* Medium post

Requirements:

* Public (no login)
* Direct link to https://documentos.legal

---

### Step 4 — Validation

* Backlink page accessible in incognito
* Link visible and clickable

---

## 5. Frequency

* Execute:

  * After each deployment batch
  * Or every 2–4 weeks

---

## 6. Rules

* Do not submit all URLs at once
* Max 5 URLs per cycle
* Prioritize high-intent pages first
* Always pair indexation with backlink creation

---

## 7. Output

Per cycle:

* 5 URLs → indexation requested
* 1–2 backlinks → live and crawlable

---

## 8. Stop Condition

* All Tier A pages indexed
* Then proceed to Tier B expansion

---

## 9. Notes

* Google indexing is non-deterministic
* Requests increase probability, not guarantee
* Backlinks accelerate discovery and prioritization
